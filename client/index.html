Collect all types of items with unique Sattributes! Earn special items, sell them or Love them.

Add adorable items

Add your item with their attributes and image.

For this demo:

  • Install MetaMask browser plugin as wallet
  • In MetaMask select a local (install Ganache) or Rinkeby testnet
  • Optional fund (faucet) your Rinkeby account
  • Select Create NFT to add a item (Image, Metadata, Chain)
  • At My NFTs select one and set Offer
  • At Catalog 'Buy me' to become the owner of a item

Note: When logged-in: always the footer must show a valid 'my address' as your MetaMask wallet address, Contract address and Network to work properly.
